# Minus AI

### A library of educational and practical resources supporting environmentally responsible AI (non-)use, specifically in human-computer-interaction (HCI).
<br>
Unfortunately, AI is currently not environmentally responsible due to the complex extractive and exploitative systems it is based on. Yet, (gen)AI use is skyrocketing in HCI research and design, without widespread consciousness or consideration of its significant negative impact on an already fragile natural environment, which concerns us all. This library aims to help educators, practitioners and users of HCI technologies better understand the environmental pitfalls of AI and find practical alternatives for more responsible HCI that reduces damage to our planet, its ecosystems and human life.

Further Resources for a broader audience can be found here:  [AI Resist List](https://airesistlist.org/)
